## Projects

### Supersonic Jet Noise Reduction
*PhD Project Work, University of Cincinnati*

**Goal:**
* Characterize the flow and acoustic behavior of both axisymmetric and non-axisymmetric nozzles.
* Implement active and passive jet noise reduction techniques.

**Methods:**
* Design and 3D print supersonic nozzles.
* Utilize experimental and numerical methods to capture the flow and acoustics of various supersonic jets.
* Apply signal processing and data analysis tools to understand the aerodynamic and acoustic behavior of the jets.

### Modeling and Simulation of Flows using CFD Tools
*P&G Digital Accelerator at the University of Cincinnati*

**Goal:**
* Study and understand flow dynamics in porous media, as well as mixing, sloshing, and capillary flows.

**Methods:**
* Utilize CFD tools including OpenFOAM, GOMA, Ansys Fluent, and FLOW-3D to analyze flow dynamics at the microscopic (fiber) level for various P&G products.
